Khoi Tran - EST 1999

I’m a designer who believes in the power of blending diverse disciplines, because who says you can’t fuse art, technology, and creativity into one? With a background that spans design, art, and computer science, I approach each project as an opportunity to bring multiple perspectives together, creating something truly unique. Based in Michigan, I’ve had the privilege of working across a range of industries, allowing me to constantly evolve and refine my approach.

Whether I’m developing innovative user interfaces or crafting visual identities that tell a compelling story, my goal is always the same: to design solutions that make an impact. My international experience has taught me the importance of context and adaptability, and it’s this mindset that informs everything I create, from the smallest details to the grandest concepts.

When I’m not immersed in a design challenge, you’ll probably find me exploring new food recipes, making too complicated art, or enjoying the serene outdoors of Michigan.

If you have a project in mind or just want to chat about the intersection of design and technology, I’d love to connect!
