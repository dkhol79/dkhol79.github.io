My House

Home Through Time

3D Modeling

Overview

This project is about creating a 3D model of my house in Vietnam, the place where I grew up. It is more than just walls and rooms. It holds memories of family meals, quiet mornings, and everyday moments that shaped who I am. want to keep those moments alive through this digital version. Each room, corner, and detail tells a story that still lives in my heart. This model is my way of holding on to the feeling of home.

Tools

  • Rhino 3D
  • Keyshot
Process

Modeling

The 3D modeling process focused on the outside of the house, including the shape of the building, the roof, and the surrounding yard. I added trees, grass, and other details to reflect the outdoor space I remember growing up in. I wanted the scene to feel familiar and peaceful, like how it felt to stand outside the house as a child. Even without the inside, the outside view already holds a strong connection to my memories.

Final Product

My House

The final product is a 3D model of the outside of my childhood home in Vietnam. It includes the house, trees, grass, and the surrounding space that I remember from growing up. The model captures the look and feel of the place, with simple details that bring back quiet moments spent outdoors.
